A post type that explicitly asks for feedback, not likes. Turns comments into structured critiques — design review, code review, writing feedback. Comments become useful, not just "great work bro."

Public roadmap / goal wall

Your goals, visible to all

Users post a public goal — "launching my SaaS in 90 days" — and their posts auto-link to it. Profile shows goal progress. Followers can cheer milestones. Turns your profile into a journey, not a highlight reel.

Reading time + depth score

Know before you click

Every post shows estimated read time and a depth score (quick take / deep dive / long read). Users choose what they have energy for. Instagram shows you everything the same — shallow or deep.

Honest reaction system

Beyond likes

Replace the like button with reactions that mean something — "learned from this," "disagree but respect," "want to try this," "saved for later." Gives creators real signal, not just vanity numbers.


Accountability circles

Small groups, real pressure

Private groups of 5–10 people who share work-in-progress with each other weekly. No public pressure, just peer accountability. Think "mastermind group" built into the platform natively.
